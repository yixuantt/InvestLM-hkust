InvestLM: A Finance Domain Large Language Model

Yi Yang
Hong Kong University of Science and Technology
Paper Code arXiv Try it

Hong Kong's first open-source LLM for financial generative AI (GenAI) applications, capable of generating investment-related, human-like responses comparable to those of well-known commercial chatbots.
